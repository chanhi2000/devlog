## 2-invest-in-code-quality"><a href="#2-invest-in-code-quality">2. Invest In Code Quality

In addition to writing clean and readable code, it's important to also
consider the overall quality of your code.

This includes things like performance, security, and maintainability.
By writing high-quality code, you'll be able to build systems that are
more reliable, scalable, and easier to maintain over time.

One way to write clean and quality code is <a href="https://youtu.be/0nVT1gM4vPg">**with the help of static code analysis**</a>.

Investing in code quality will pay dividends in the later stages of any project.

---

## 3-work-on-complex-systems"><a href="#3-work-on-complex-systems">3. Work On Complex Systems

If you aspire to be senior engineer or software architect, you have to
work on complex systems. You need to be in a position to solve the toughest problems.

What do I consider to be a <a href="https://youtu.be/Ru6_b50wdfo">**complex system**</a>?

That's difficult to say, but here are some rough guidelines:

- Microservices
<li>Event-driven systems
<li>High performance systems

Of course, you don't need a <a href="https://youtu.be/Ru6_b50wdfo">**fancy architecture**</a> to work in a complex system.

If you are working in a business domain with many domain rules,
I consider that a complex system also.

You should strive to always be in a position to work on a challenging project,
this will help you grow.

---

## 4-be-comfortable-in-the-cloud"><a href="#4-be-comfortable-in-the-cloud">4. Be Comfortable In The Cloud

The cloud is here to stay, and you should be familiar with at least one
of the major cloud providers:

- Microsoft Azure
<li>Amazon Web Services
<li>Google Cloud Platform

Most of them give you free credits to get started and explore the services they offer.

I stayed away from <a href="https://youtu.be/QP0pi7xe24s">**cloud development**</a> for too long in my career, and now I wish I started sooner.

You want to be <a href="https://youtu.be/QP0pi7xe24s">**comfortable in the cloud**</a> to be a better software engineer.

---

## 5-take-care-of-yourself"><a href="#5-take-care-of-yourself">5. Take Care Of Yourself

Being a software engineer is mentally and physically demanding, and it's
important to take care of yourself in order to maintain a healthy work-life balance.

This includes getting enough sleep, eating well, and taking breaks when needed.

Taking care of yourself can also help you stay focused and productive in your work.

I like to take short breaks every hour or so, and not think about work for a few minutes.
This helps me replenish my energy, and allows me to continue to operate on a high level.
